<h3>Cookie Notice:</h3>  

<p>A cookie is a text file placed on the hard drive of a computer by some Web pages or Web servers. Cookies cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. They are uniquely assigned to a visitor and can only be read by a Web server in the domain that issued the cookie. You can accept cookies (generally their browser's default setting) or decline them through your browser settings. Persistent cookies remain on a computer's hard drive until the visitor deletes them. Session cookies are deleted automatically when you close your browser. In compliance with Information Commissioners Office, the openoutcomes.apperta.org site does not set persistent cookies on visitors' computers. </p>
